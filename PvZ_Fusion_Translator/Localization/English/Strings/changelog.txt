2. New Zombies
- Trident Gargantuar

3. Almanac Update
- There is a completely revamped Almanac now available, including a search function and updated organization and visuals! (You can also press C in a level while hovering over a plant to instantly view their Almanac entry.)

4. New Gacha Mode - Odyssey Gacha: Rerolled
- In this mode, basic plants will appear on the conveyor belt. The number below indicates how many times the plant can be planted. Random modifiers may also appear on the conveyor belt!

5. I, Zombie Changes
- A brand new Lane Arena
- All zombies can now be placed in I, Zombie Sandbox.

6. New Skins
- Aristocattail > Halloween Aristocattail
- Magnetar > Magician Magnetar

7. Zombie Adjustments
- Ultra Mecha-nut
  - HP: 90,000 → 300,000
  - Damage Cap: 100 → 300
  - Damage Absorption: Zombies will now take a forced 1 damage per hit when Ultra Mecha-nut absorbs damage.
- Element-infused Gargantuars
  - Modifiers:
    - All 4 Gargantuars will gain the same abilities.
    - All 4 Gargantuars will gain the same immunities.

8. Chibi Bosses
- Purchase modifiers to unlock Abyssal Chibi or Mini Striker in your next Odyssey runs for some extra help!
