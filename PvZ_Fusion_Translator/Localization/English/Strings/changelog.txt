<align=center><size=130%>Plants VS. Zombies: Fusion - Update 3.1.1 Changelog</size></align>

1. Abyss Season 2
- Test your skills in the brand new Abyss season, featuring 30 new levels, with a new level layout and Abyss modifiers! 
- Retain your setup throughout your run and upgrade it after each level by choosing new modifiers. You can favor which buffs you want in the new Abyss almanac.
